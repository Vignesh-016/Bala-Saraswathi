THE WORKING MODEL

A simple, transparent process that guides you from enrolment to completion ensuring easy contributions, fair auctions, instant disbursements, and rewarding savings.

✔

STEP 1

Enrolment

Join easily by contacting Bala Saraswathi Chits or visiting any branch. Select a chit plan tailored to your financial needs.

✔

STEP 2

CONTRIBUTION

Make regular fixed contributions each month or week, ensuring flexibility and convenience.

✔

STEP 3

BIDDING/AUCTION

In each cycle, members join an auction to bid for the pooled funds. The member with the lowest bid wins the lump sum, and the remaining amount is distributed to others as dividends.

✔

STEP 4

DISBURSEMENT

Spot payment is our specialty—funds are transferred instantly to the winning member for immediate access.

✔

STEP 5

COMPLETION

Once every member has received their share, the chit cycle concludes. You can then renew your journey or select a new plan to continue benefiting.
