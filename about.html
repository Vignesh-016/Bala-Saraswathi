About US

Your money, your trust, our promise.

Who we are

Bala Saraswathi Chits Private Limited, built on trust, transparency, and community empowerment, started as Balu Chit Funds in 1991 and became Bala Saraswathi Chits in 2007. With over 35 years of experience, we proudly serve 50,000+ members across multiple branches.
We focus on helping individuals, families, and business owners achieve their financial goals through innovative savings and lending solutions. By combining ethical practices with modern service, we build lasting relationships and guide members toward financial security.

Mission

To offer trustworthy, efficient, and customer-centric chit fund services that empower members and foster long-term financial stability.

Vision

To be the leading chit fund partner—delivering secure, transparent solutions and enriching the lives of individuals, families, and entrepreneurs as they pursue their ambitions.
