Is my money safe? −
Yes. We operate under government guidelines and maintain transparent records. All contributions are securely managed.
How are members selected for payouts? +
Through a fair, open auction process where the highest bidder receives the payout each cycle.
What happens if I miss a payment? +
Our team assists with options to catch up or adjust your contributions flexibly.
Can I withdraw early? +
Early withdrawal terms vary by scheme. Please contact support for details specific to your plan.
Are there any joining fees?? +
Some schemes require a nominal registration fee. All charges are clearly outlined before joining.
How does the auction process work? +
Members bid discounts on the pooled amount; the highest discount bidder wins the prize money.
Who manages the chit fund? +
A licensed organizer collects contributions, conducts auctions, distributes payouts, and ensures regulatory compliance.
